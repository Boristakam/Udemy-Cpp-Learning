protected members are accessible WITHIN the base class itself, and WITHIN its derived classes
not accessible by any objects (whether from base or derived classes) 

behavave just like private members but unlike private members they are accessible via inheritance
    Note: 
        Public inheritance: is-a inheritance
            in the Derived class, inherited public members remain public, inherited protected members remain protected
            and private members are not inherited (NO ACCESS)
        Protected inheritance: 
            in the Derived class, inherited public members become protected, inherited protected members remain protected
            and private members are not inherited (NO ACCESS)
        Private inheritance: 
            in the Derived class, inherited public members become private, inherited protected members become private
            and private members are not inherited (NO ACCESS)
